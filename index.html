FAQ

Quick answers.

What makes Wave different?

Wave helps you connect with your city. See where the energy is, where to skip, and what's actually happening — not curated content. Real moments, honest callouts, and authentic vibes from people who are there.

What kind of content do people share?

Funny clips, chaotic moments, honest callouts, and real vibes from venues. People share what's actually happening — the good, the bad, and the chaotic. It's unfiltered and authentic.

Can I share anonymously?

Yes. Wave has anonymous commenting and sharing features. Share honest takes, callouts, or moments without the pressure of being identified. Your insights help others make better decisions about their night.

How do I know where to go?

See where people are gathering, check the vibe through real clips, and read honest takes from people who are there. Wave gives you the social intel to know where the energy is and where to avoid.

Is this just for college campuses?

Wave works anywhere people go out. Connect with your city — whether that's a college campus or a major metro. See where the energy is and make better decisions about your night.
