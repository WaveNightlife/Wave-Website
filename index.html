FAQ

Quick answers.

Is Wave only for college?

It’s built for the pace of campus nights — but it works anywhere people go out.

Do I need to post to use it?

No. Post when you want. Wave still works if you’re just checking the feed.

What makes this different from Instagram stories?

Stories are scattered and late. Wave is organized around the night, live.

How do venues fit in?

Wave is about what the night feels like. Venues can show what’s happening when it matters.
